## Abstract (snapshot)
This preprint explores **polynomial sheaf diffusion** paired with **equivariant sheaf neural networks** to model structured data on graphs. The approach leverages sheaf-theoretic operators to encode consistency and symmetry, providing a principled way to diffuse information while respecting local geometry.
